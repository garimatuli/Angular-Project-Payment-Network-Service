1. Users can register with TIJN by providing their Name, SSN, Password, Email Address or Phone Number. Also name and password can be updated.

2. Users can link multiple email and/or phone number with their account and login using any of them. Users are also able to unlink their email/phone if they are not currently logged in with that particular email/phone number.

3. Users can link multiple bank accounts with their account but they should define one of them as the primary funding source. Users are also able to unlink a bank account if its not their primary funding source.

4. Users can send money by specifying other user's email address or phone number, amount and a memo. In cases when sent amount is over senderâ€™s TIJN balance, the entire payment will be funded from the primary bank account.

5. Users can request money from multiple users by specifying their user's email address or phone number, total bill amount, the percentage split of the amount and a memo.

6. Users can view the total amount sent or received in a particular time period. User can also view all transactions with a specific recipient ie another TIJN User.
